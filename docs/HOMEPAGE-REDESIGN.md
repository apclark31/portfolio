## Problem
After Phase 3's multi-page split, the homepage duplicates content from `/experience` and `/projects`. The 2-entry timeline preview and full projects bento feel redundant rather than curated.

## Keep
- **Hero** — Full-viewport section with headline, portrait, stat card, CTAs
- **BentoIntro** — "Balance of Precision & Passion" + 12+ years stat
- **FloatingGallery** — "Curated Works" horizontal scroll
- **CoreCompetencies** — Bento grid (CRO, Technical Feats, Interface Design, Strategic Thinking)
- **About** — "The Human Behind The Code" with portrait + bento cards
- **Contact** — Split layout with info panel + form
- **Footer**

## Replace
- **Timeline preview (current: 2 entries from TimelinePlaceholder)** → Simple CTA section. Something like "12+ years across Fender, SOREL, Dr. Martens..." with a prominent link to `/experience`. No timeline cards — just a compelling summary that draws you in.
- **Projects bento (current: full ProjectsSection duplicated)** → Visual hook + link to `/projects`. Not the full grid — a teaser that makes you want to click through.

## Goal
The homepage should feel like an editorial landing page — unique content that introduces who Alex is and what he does, with smart CTAs pulling visitors into the detail pages. Not a condensed copy of those pages.
